# A list of Questions to NotebookLM for Pre-Reading the Book, and figuring the Big Picture.

```
Help me understand : “What is this book about as a whole?”.
Discover and Explain the leading theme, main point, and orderly subdivision of the book.
```
---
```
Identify the author's most important keywords and interpret them to arrive at a precise understanding of how the author uses them.
List the meanings for Important Terms used in this book.
```
---
```
One Word can represent several terms.
Make a list of Words that distinguish the Authors Vocabulary and his Terminology,
```
---
```
List the Words and Terms Defined Explicitly, or in a specific way in the book.
```
---
```
Help me understand : “What is being said in detail, and how?”.
Explain and List the author's main ideas, assertions, and arguments.
For the Important propositions made, what are the Reasons provided.
What are Authors most important sentences.

```
---
```
Help me understand : “What of it?” for this Book. Explain the book's significance, implications, and relevance.
What are the actionable insights from this book.
```
---
```
Help me understand : “The Unity Core Idea of this Book”. State what the whole book is about with the utmost brevity.
Summarize the book's main point or overarching theme in a single sentence or a short paragraph. 
```
---
```
Help to : “Enumerate this book's major parts” and "Principal Divisions". 
Outline the book's major parts in their order and relation, explaining how they logically connect to each other and contribute to the overall argument.
This involves breaking down the book's structure beyond the table of contents to reveal its logical architecture.
```
---
```
Define the problem or problems the author is trying to solve.
Identify the core question(s) that drove the author to write the book and put them in an intelligible order.
Assess whether the author successfully answered the questions defined in section “Define the problem“.
Does  the author knows which problems remained unsolved.
```
---
```
What are the Key Declarations being made.
What are the Key Interrogations being made.
```
---
```
What are key Affirmations made by Author.
What are key Denials made by Author.

```
---
```
Determine which of his problems the author has solved, and which he has not.
What are the Authors Solutions.
```
---
```
In Answering and Solving the Questions, does the Author raise new Questions, and UnAnswered Questions. What are they.

```
---
```
Establish a neutral, controlling terminology for the subject.
Translate each author's specialized terms and concepts into this common vocabulary, effectively forcing them to "use simpler language".
Frame a set of neutral commonsense questions that are central to inquiry.
Map out the intellectual issues by identifying questions that receive conflicting answers.

```
---
```
List the Quotes made by the Author.
List the Anecdotes made by the Author.
```
---
```
List the Metaphors and Examples used in this book.
```
---
```
List and describe in brief the Stories explained/used used by the Author to prove a point/explain a concept.
```
